This app does not require a login or demo account.

Core review flow:
1. Launch the app.
2. Allow location permission when prompted, or search for a Singapore postal code such as 238801.
3. The app loads LTA DataMall EV charging data and shows nearby charging points on Apple Maps.
4. Tap "Directions in Maps" to open Apple Maps navigation.

The app does not collect user account data and does not provide account creation.
